Ignition Lock Cable: Adjustments

NOTE: Locking cable must always be adjusted in installation position.

- Shift selector lever into "1" position.
- Turn ignition key to right (starting position) and release.





- Adjust clearance to 0.7 mm between lever -1- and locking pin -2- by sliding locking cable casing.
Dimension a = 0.7 mm
- Tighten locking cable anchorage screw in this position to 6 Nm (53 inch lb).
- Shift selector lever into "P" position and turn ignition key to end position.
- Pull out ignition key.
- It must only be possible to remove ignition key in this selector lever position.
- Selector lever cannot be shifted out of "P" position.
