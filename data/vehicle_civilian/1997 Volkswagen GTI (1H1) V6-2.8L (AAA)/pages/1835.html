Front Seat, Remove and Install (Vehicles With Side Airbags)



Front seat, removing and installing (vehicles with side airbags)





Note:

Before disconnecting ignition and Ground (GND) wire, the technician must be electrostatically discharged (touch closing wedge for door or chassis briefly)

Connector -2- (at VAS 5061 airbag adapter) is not required.

- Disconnect ignition and Ground (GND) wire from side airbag.

- Connect connector -1- (at VAS5O61 airbag adapter) in connector housing -4-.

- Connect connector -3- (at VAS5O61 airbag adapter) in connector housing -5-.

- Disconnect harness connectors for seat heating and seat adjustment if equipped.

Installing





Installation is reverse of removal.

- Check glide pieces -6- (left and right) and front glide piece -5- for damage, replace if necessary.

- Lubricate glide pieces -6- (left and right) and front glide piece (multi- purpose grease).

Note:

- If the Airbag Malfunction Indicator Lamp (MIL) - K75- indicates a malfunction following seat installation, use the VAG1551 to erase DTC memory and check it again.
