Fuse/Relay Panel, Removing and Installing

CAUTION: Service Precautions

NOTE: The fuse/relay panel is located under the left side of the instrument panel.

Removal and Installation:




- Remove lower instrument panel trim.
- Spread retaining clips -1- and disengage lock pins -2- from brackets -4-.
- Swing retaining clips -1- forward and pull from fuse/relay panel pins -3-.
- Spread left retaining bracket -4- and disengage from pin -5-.
- Lift fuse/relay panel from brackets -4-.
- Remove multi-point connectors from back of fuse/relay panel and remove fuse/relay panel.
- Install in reverse order of removal. Make sure pin -5- and retaining pins -2- engage recesses in brackets -4-.
