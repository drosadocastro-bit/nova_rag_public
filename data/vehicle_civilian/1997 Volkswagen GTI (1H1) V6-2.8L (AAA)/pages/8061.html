Front Door - With Lower Side-Impact Member (Aluminum)

Group: 57

Number: 96-02

Date: Sept. 10, 1996

File this Technical Bulletin in Repair Manual No.: W42 011 294 114A

Subject:
Front Door with Lower Side-impact Member (Aluminum)

Model(s):
Golf, GTI, Jetta 1997 -->

For m.y. 1997, the front door assembly now includes a lower side-impact member made of aluminum, as well as the original steel (upper) side-impact member.
