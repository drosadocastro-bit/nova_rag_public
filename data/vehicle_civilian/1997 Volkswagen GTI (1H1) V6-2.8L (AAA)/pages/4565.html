Remove and Install Timing Chain Tensioner

Remove and Install Timing Chain Tensioner





Remove by unscrewing from camshaft sprocket cover.

Install tensioner and tighten to 30 Nm (22 ft lb)

Note: If the chain tensioner has been pulled apart, bleed chain tensioner before installing.
