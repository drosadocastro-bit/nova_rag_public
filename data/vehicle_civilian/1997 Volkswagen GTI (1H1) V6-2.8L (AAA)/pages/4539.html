Rear Crankshaft Main Bearing Seal: Service and Repair

NOTE: During removal and installation procedures, refer to the component location illustration above and component notes below.

COMPONENT NOTES
Vibration Damper Bolt (1)
- Tighten to: 100 Nm (74 ft. lbs.) + 1/4 turn (90°).

Sealing Flange (Cover) Bolt (3)
- Tighten to: 10 Nm (7 ft. lbs.).

Front Sealing Flange (Front Cover) (5)
- Coat sealing surfaces with sealer part No. AMV 188 001 02, or equivalent.

Rear Sealing Flange (Rear Cover) (7)
- Coat sealing surfaces with sealer part No. AMV 188 001 02, or equivalent.

Rear Oil Seal (8)
- Remove using removal tool No. 2036 or equivalent.
- Lightly oil sealing lip.
- Install over sleeve tool No. 2003/2A, or equivalent.
- Push in onto stop with installer tool No. 2003/3, or equivalent.

Flywheel/Drive Plate (9)
- When removing and installing, counter holder tool No. VW 553, or equivalent.
- Install drive plate.

Flywheel/Drive Plate Bolts (10)
- Tighten to: 60 Nm (44 ft. lbs.) + 1/4 turn (90°).
- Always replace.
