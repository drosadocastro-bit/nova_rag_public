Fog/Driving Lamp: Adjustments





Adjust fog lights by turning adjusting screw -arrow- counter clockwise to lower beam or clockwise to raise beam. For procedure to gain access to screws, Service and Repair

NOTE: Horizontal (side-to-side) fog light aim is fixed and can not be adjusted.
