Remove/Install






NOTE: Refer to Planetary Gearbox Disassembly/Assembly

COMPONENT NOTES AND INFORMATION
1 Bolt
- Quantity used, 7
- Tighten to: 8 Nm (70 inch lb) plus an additional 1/4 turn (90°)
- The additional turn of 90° may be done in several stages
2 ATF pump with -B2- piston
- Refer to ATF Pump Disassembly/Assembly
3 O-ring
- Always replace
- Place on ATF pump
4 Gasket
- Always replace
5 Spring cap
- Quantity used, 6
- Install 3 spring caps after installing first outer plate
- Install 3 spring caps before installing last outer plate
6 Spring
- Quantity used, 3
7 Corrugated spring washer
8 Outer plate -B2-
- Quantity. Application and ID
- Determining thickness of outer plate, refer to Adjusting 2nd and 4th gear brake -B2-. Adjustments
9 Inner plate -B2-
- Quantity. Application and ID
- Before installing place new inner plates in ATF for 15 minutes
10 Outer plate -B2-
- Always install 2 mm thick outer plates
- Quantity. Application and ID
11 Outer plate -B2-
- Fit 3 mm thick outer plate to supporting tube
12 Supporting tube -B2-
- For the -B2- plate package
- Length of -B2- with:
5 inner plates 68.6 mm
6 inner plates 64.9 mm
- Quantity of -B2- inner plates. Application and ID
- Install so that groove engages in wedge of free wheel
13 Transmission housing with clutches installed
