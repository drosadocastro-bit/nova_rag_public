Engine Lubrication: Service Precautions


Note:
^ If quantities of mete/ particles or other material (caused, for example, by crankshaft seizure or connecting rod bearings) are found in the engine oil during repairs, thoroughly clean the oil passages and replace the oil cooler to prevent future damage from occurring later.
^ The oil level must not be above the Max. mark (Danger of damage to catalyst!)
