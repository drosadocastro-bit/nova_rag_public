Rear Dome Type Treble Speakers, Removing and Installing



Rear dome type treble speakers, removing and installing

Note: The dome type treble speakers can only be replaced together with the carrier plate.


Removing:

- First remove woofer.





- Remove three screws (arrows) from carrier plate.

- Remove carrier plate from side panel.

- Disconnect connector.


Installing

- Installation is reverse of removal.
