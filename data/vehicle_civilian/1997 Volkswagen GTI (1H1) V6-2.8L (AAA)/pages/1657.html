Cassette Storage Box Light Bulb, Removing and Installing







Cassette storage box light bulb, removing and installing


Note: Cassette storage box is not installed on all vehicles.

- Remove cassette storage box.

- Carefully pry off cover at (arrows).

- Remove light bulb.

- Install in reverse order of removal.
