Security Lamp/Indicator: Service and Repair

CAUTION: Before working on electrical system disconnect battery ground (GND) strap.

Alarm Indicator Light -K95-:





Removing:
- Remove door trim.
- Disconnect 2-pin connector.
- Release warning lamp lower section from locating device and pull out.
- Pull upper section out of door trim.
