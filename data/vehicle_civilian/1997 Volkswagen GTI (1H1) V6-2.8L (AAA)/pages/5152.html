Data Link Connector: Description and Operation

Resistor For Controling Electronic Interference

NOTE: Vehicles with automatic transmission only.

Jetta from VM 049 899
Golf from VM 048 289

Controls electronic interference between control modules through Data Link Connector (DLC) circuit.





A Connector (Black)
B Connector (red or white)
1 Adaptor housing for TV connectors
2 Data Link Connector (DLC) junction box (T44)
3 Fuse/relay panel
4 Resistor
