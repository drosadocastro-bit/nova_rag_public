Without Air Conditioning

Removing And Installing Fresh Air Blower Series Resistance With Fuse -N24-:




- Disconnect harness connectors from series resistance -N24-.
- Squeeze locking tabs and remove series resistance.

NOTE: When installing, if the locking tabs do not engage, fasten the series resistance unit with a self-tapping screw through the mounting hole (arrow).

- Install in reverse order of removal.
