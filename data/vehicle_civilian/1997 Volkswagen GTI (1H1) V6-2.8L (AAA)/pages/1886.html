Instrument Cluster / Carrier: Application and ID

Running Change in Production, 01.96:





The airbag Malfunction Indicator Lamp (MIL) has been relocated to the instrument cluster. A low-fuel indicator warning light has also been added.

A- Airbag Malfunction Indicator Lamp (MIL)
- Now located in instrument cluster
- Stays on after the ignition is switched on, or comes on while driving to indicate a malfunction.
B- Low fuel indicator warning light
- Less than 1.6 gallons: ON
- At least 2.4 gallons: OFF
- 1.6 to 2.4 gallons: may be either on or off





1- Low fuel indicator warning light
2- Airbag MIL
A- Diesel engine version
B- Gasoline engine version
