Cylinder Head Gasket: Service and Repair





PROCEDURE
- Remove old sealant from 3 mm holes in the cylinder head gasket-arrows-.
- Fill 3 mm holes in the cylinder head gasket with sealant part No. AMV 1 88 001 02, or equivalent.

NOTE: With the cylinder head installed the holes are only half visible.
