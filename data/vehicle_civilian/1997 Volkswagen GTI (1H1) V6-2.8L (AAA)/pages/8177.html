Instrument Panel Bulb: Locations

NOTES:
- The instrument cluster should not be disassembled.
- For checking instrument cluster voltage supply, indicators and warning lights using VAG 1598 test box. Testing and Inspection
- For other electrical system and component troubleshooting procedures see Electrical Wiring Diagrams, Testing and Inspection and Component Locations.





1- Bulb with holder
- Instrument cluster light -L10-
2- Bulb with holder
- Right turn signal indicator light -K94-
3- Bulb with holder
- Headlight high beam indicator light -K1-
4- Bulb with holder
- Left turn signal indicator light -K65-
5- Socket 1 for 28-pin connector -T28-
6- Bulb with holder
- Digital display light -L75-, illumination for digital clock or multi-function indicator
7- Bulbs with holders
- For indicators and/or warning lights: applications depend on vehicle equipment
8- Bulb with holder
- Illumination for speedometer -G21- and odometer
9- Socket 2 for 28-pin connector -T28b-

- For numbers 5 and 9, Connector Views
