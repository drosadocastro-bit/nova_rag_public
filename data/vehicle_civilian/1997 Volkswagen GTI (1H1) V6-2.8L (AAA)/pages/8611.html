Wipers - Intermittent Function Deleted

Group: 92

Number: 97-02

Date: Aug. 12, 1997

Subject:
Programmable Intermittent Wiper, Function Deleted

Model(s):
Golf, Jetta 1997 -->

Condition

Intermittent wiper function not programmable.

Beginning m.y 1997, the programmable intermittent wiper relay has been replaced with a non-programmable relay.

Service

- Condition is normal, NO action is required.

If a customer asks about this function, please direct him/her to the wipers section in their Owner's Manual (Literature No: as listed below) for an explanation of how their system functions:

Model Literature Number

Golf 973.551.1H1.21
Jetta 973.551.1H2.21
