Cargo Lamp Switch: Service and Repair

Removing and Installing Luggage Compartment Light Switch -F5- (Not Applicable to USA/CAN):

NOTE: The luggage compartment light switch -F5- is located on the rear hatch/trunk lid lock assembly

Removing:
- Remove rear hatch/trunk lid lock assembly.
- Remove switch connector.
- Use screwdriver to disengage retaining tabs and pull switch from lock assembly.

Installing:
- Install switch into lock assembly until retaining tabs engage.
- Install switch connector.
- Install lock assembly.
