Front Door Impact Padding, 2 Door





1- Upper and lower impact bars.
2- Impact padding, support.
- To install, fit onto impact bar and slide to rear onto stop (lock reinforcement).
3- Lower impact padding.
- Remove cover from adhesive.
- If impact padding had been removed and padding will no longer stick to door skin, this can be reworked with butyl cord (AKL 450 005 05)
- Fit impact padding onto lower impact bar and slide to rear support -2-.
4- Wedge
- Secure wedge between upper impact bar and lower impact padding.
