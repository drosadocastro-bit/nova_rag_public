Rear Seat Belt

Rear Seat Belts, Removing And Installing:





1 - Belt height adjustment fitting
2 - Belt reel
- Removing and installing: See procedure below.
3 - Clasp (2-door)
- Secured to side trim panel with three spring nuts
4 - Rear belt anchor
- Tightening torque (hex bolt): 40 Nm (30 ft lb)
5 - Lap belt
- Tightening torque (hex bolt):40 Nm (30 ft lb)
6 - Double latch
- Tightening torque (hex bolt): 40 Nm (30 ft lb)
- Removing and installing: See procedure below.

Removing And Installing Belt Reel:
Remove, the side panel trim from panel, side liner for hat rack and the C-pillar trim.





1 - Locating lug
- Serves to position the belt reel
2 - Hex head bolt
- Tightening torque: 40 Nm (30 ft lb)
3 - Belt reel
- Upper part hooked into side panel

Removing And Installing Lap Belt And Double Latch:





1 - Hex bolts
- Tightening torque: 40 Nm (30 ft lb)
2 - Caps
3 - Lap belt
4 - Double latch
