Rear Tweeter (4-Door), Removing and Installing



Rear tweeter (4-door, except Jetta from 1996 m.y.), removing and installing





1 - Door release lever trim

2 - Tweeter

- Carefully remove door release lever trim -1- (with tweeter -2-).

- Remove connector.

- Install in reverse order of removal.
