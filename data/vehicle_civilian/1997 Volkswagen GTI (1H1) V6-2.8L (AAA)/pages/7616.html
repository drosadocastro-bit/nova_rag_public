Speaker: Locations

NOTE: The deluxe and premium stereo systems use front and rear passive (non-amplified) speakers.





Front Speakers:

Bass speakers woofers):
Left front woofer -R21- and right front woofer -R23-
- Mounted on front door panel
- Diameter: 168 mm (6.6 in.)

Treble speakers (tweeters):
Left front tweeter -R20- and right front tweeter -R22-
- Dome tweeter
- Mounted on base in instrument panel
- Diameter: 100 mm (3.9 in.)

Rear Speakers:

Bass speakers (woofers):
Left rear woofer -R15- and right rear woofer -R17-
- 4-door mounted on rear door panel
- 2-door - mounted on rear side panel
- Diameter: 168 mm (6.6 in.)

Treble speakers (tweeters):
Right rear tweeter -R16- and left rear tweeter -R14-
- Dome tweeter
- Connected to rear base speaker
- 4-door - mounted near door lever
- 2-door - mounted on rear side panel
