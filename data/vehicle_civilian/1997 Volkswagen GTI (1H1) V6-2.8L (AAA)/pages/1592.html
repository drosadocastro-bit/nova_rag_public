Telescoping Antenna -R11-



Removing:





- Remove Ground (GND) strap from hood and left wheel housing.
- Disconnect antenna cable connector located behind lower left A-pillar side trim.
- Pull antenna cable into plenum.
- Remove left wheel housing liner.
- Remove bracket (arrow) from antenna.
- Pull antenna downward and remove.
- Remove fender grommet.

Installing:
- Install in reverse order of removal.
- Hold fender grommet in position and push antenna into grommet.

1- GND point, hood
2- GND point, left wheel housing
