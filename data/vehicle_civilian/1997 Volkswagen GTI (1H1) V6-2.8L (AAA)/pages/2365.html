Multi-Pin Connector I, Part 3, Blue



Radio connector (Sound System), terminal identification

Multi-pin connector I, -T20-, consists of 3 separate connector parts, each designated by a different color.



Multi-pin connector I, part 3, blue





13 - CD-changer, DATA IN

14 - CD-changer, DATA OUT

15 - CD-changer, CLOCK

16 - CD-changer, power supply (+), terminal 30

17 - CD-changer, control signal

18 - CD-changer, left and right port, Ground (GND)

19 - CD-changer, left port, CD/L

20 - CD-changer, right port, CD/R
