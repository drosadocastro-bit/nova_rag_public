M/T Case - Sealing Precautions

Group: 34

Number: 97-06

Date: Nov. 10, 1997

Subject:
Transmission Case, Sealing

Model(s):
All with 020 transmission m.y. All

Production





As of transmission build date 06035, Most transmission cases have been sealed using sealant AMV188 200 03 instead of a paper gasket between case halves at -arrows-.





Code letters and date of manufacture -A-.

Service
You may encounter instances after this transmission build date, where a paper gasket Part No: 020 301 191F has been used.

CAUTION
Pre-load adjustment on bearings will be affected if correct sealing method is not used.

DO NOT install a paper gasket when a transmission has been sealed with sealant.

DO NOT use sealant AMV188 200 03 on transmission that has been sealed with a paper gasket.

CAUTION
Part numbers are for reference only. Always check with your Parts Dept. for the latest parts information.
