Camshaft Alignment Tool - AST Tool # 3391




Camshaft Alignment Tool

AST tool# 3391





Used to hold both camshafts in T.D.C. position. Applicable to VW and Audi V6, 2.8L and 2.7L engines with 5 valves per cylinder. Used in conjunction with 3242. For the 3.0 V6 used in Audi models see #T 40030

- Used for Camshaft Alignment
- Applicable to VW and Audi
- Used in conjunction with #3242
- Made in U.S.A.
