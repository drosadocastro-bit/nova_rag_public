M/T - Shifter Difficult to Operate

Group: 34

Number: 98-01

Date: Apr. 22, 1998

Subject:
Shifter, Difficult to Operate

Model(s):
Golf, Jetta with 020 Transmission --> 26087 (trans. build date)

Condition

Transmission difficult to shift or will not shift into gear.

May be caused by a broken selector shaft locking bolt.





Production

New selector shaft locking bolt installed as of transmission build date 26087 (see below).





Code letters and date of manufacture (arrow).

Service

If transmission build date is before 26087:





- Remove and inspect selector shaft locking bolt -arrow-.

If broken, remove all pieces and install new locking bolt Part No: 020 301 241H.

CAUTION!
Part numbers are for reference only. Always check with your Parts Dept. for the latest parts information.
