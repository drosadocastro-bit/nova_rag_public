Seat Belts, Inspecting

Seat belts, inspecting

WARNING! After an accident in which one or more belt tensioners have been activated, both seat belts and tensioners must be replaced and the seat belt system must be inspected.

Check points:

- Check belt webbing.

- Check inertia reel (locking effect).

- Visually check belt lock.

- Check belt lock function.

- Check belt guides and lock tongue.

- Check securing parts and anchor points.

- Check lap belt automatic retractor.

Note: If customer refuses to have a damaged seat belt replaced, appropriate note should be made.
