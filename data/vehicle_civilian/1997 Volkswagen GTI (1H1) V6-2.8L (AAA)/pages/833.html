Identification of Transmission

IDENTIFICATION OF TRANSMISSION

The "01M" 4-speed automatic transmission is installed in the Golf III, Jetta III and Cabrio, beginning in the 1995 model year, in combination with either a 4-cylinder or 6-cylinder engine.
