Rear Woofer (2-Door), Removing and Installing



Rear woofer (2-door), removing and installing

Removing





- Using screwdriver, carefully pry off speaker cover.

- Remove four screws and pull speaker from side panel.

- Disconnect speaker harness connector.


Installing

- Install in reverse order of removal noting installation marks on speaker.

- Install speaker cover according to curvature of side panel.
