Cruise Control Servo: Adjustments





1- Throttle control element
2- Adjusting sleeve
3- Rod
4- Accelerator pedal

- Remove adjusting sleeve -2- from throttle control element -1- and push fully forward.
- Adjust accelerator pedal cable.
- Pull adjusting sleeve to rear until there is maximum play of 1 mm (0.04 in.) and lock by turning clockwise onto stop.
