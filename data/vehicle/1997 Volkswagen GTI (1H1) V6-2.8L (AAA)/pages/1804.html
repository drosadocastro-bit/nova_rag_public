Removal


Sunroof Headliner Removal

^ Sunroof glass panel and trim frame removed
^ Sunroof unit in "closed position"




- Remove screws that mounts slide - 1 - to headliner (one side only) and pull out of carrier unit guide channel.




- Release spring-1-and pull headliner forward out of roof cutout (headliner must be angled for removal).

Note:

When installed, spring - 1- is difficult to see.
