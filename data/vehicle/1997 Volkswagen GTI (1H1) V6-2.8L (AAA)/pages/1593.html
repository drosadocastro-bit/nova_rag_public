Fixed Antenna



11.95 on:





A fixed antenna is installed on 1996 Golf and Jetta models as of Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) 3VW_ _ _ _ _ _ TM 048514.
1- Fixed antenna
2- Antenna base
3- Grommet

Removing:
- Turn antenna -1- counterclockwise (arrow) to disconnect from threaded base -2-.
- Pull antenna out of grommet -3-.

Installing:
- Install in reverse order of removal.
