PROCEDURES


Fig. 1 Spring pin (arrow), driving out and in driving out
- Driving out:
Drive spring pin for engaging lever and spring pin for detent segment out only far enough to remove selector shaft.

NOTE: Ensure that spring pin of engaging lever does not drop into planetary gearbox.

- Driving in:
Drive in spring pins after installing selector shaft, engaging lever and detent segment.


Fig. 2 Selector shaft with selector segments, removing and installing
- With valve body installed do not damage the conductor strip.
Remove selector segment spring -A-.
Remove screw with washer (arrow).
- The washer prevents selector shaft movement.
