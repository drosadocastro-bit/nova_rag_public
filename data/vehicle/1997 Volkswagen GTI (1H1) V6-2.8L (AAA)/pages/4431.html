Camshaft: Specifications

DIMENSIONS
Axial Clearence 0.15 mm (0.0060 inch)
Radial Clearence 0.1 mm (0.0039 inch)
Maximum Runout 0.01 mm (0.0004 inch)


TIGHTENING SPECIFICATIONS
Bearing Cap Nuts 20 Nm (15 ft. lbs.)
Cam Sprocket Bolt 100 Nm (74 ft. lbs.)

NOTE: Counter Hold Camshaft with a suitable 24 mm wrench when tightening sprocket bolts.
