Cargo Lamp: Service and Repair

Removing and Installing Luggage Compartment Light -W3- (Not Applicable to USA/CAN):

Removing:





- Use screwdriver to carefully pry out luggage compartment light -W3-.
- Remove wire connector.
- Replace bulb (12 V/3 W) as necessary.

Installing:
- Install wire connector.
- Install luggage compartment light -W3- and push in until secure.
