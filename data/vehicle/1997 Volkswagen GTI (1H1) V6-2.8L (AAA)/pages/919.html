01M Transmission

COMPONENTS TESTED BY ON-BOARD DIAGNOSTICS

NOTE: If the Engine Control Module (ECM) or Transmission Control Module (TCM) is replaced, or if the repairs are performed on the Throttle Position (TP) Sensor; the system must be restored to the basic setting.





1. Transmission Control Module (TCM) -J2I7-

Location: Transmission Control Module (TCM) -J217- is located under the rear seat.

2. Engine Control Module (ECM)

Location: Engine Control Module is located in right plenum chamber.

3. Data Link Connector (DLC)

Location: The Data Link Connectors (DLC) are located behind the cover next to the ashtray.

4. Valve Body

Location: Valve Body is bolted to underside of the transmission housing inside the oil pan.

^ The Solenoid Valves -N88-, -N89-, -N90-, -N91-, -N92-, -N93-, -N94- are attached to the valve body.

5. Conductor Strip with Fluid Temperature Sensor

Location: The conductor strip is located in the oil pan on the valve body.

^ The conductor strip can be changed with the transmission installed without removing the valve body.
^ Do not kink or damage the conductor strip.

6. Multi-Function Transmission Range (TR) Switch -F125-

Location: At rear of gearbox.

7. Transmission Vehicle Speed Sensor (VSS) -G38-

Location: On top of transmission.

^ Do not interchange connector T2 for Vehicle Speed Sensors -G38- and -G68-.

8. Vehicle Speed Sensor (VSS) -G68-

Location: On top of transmission.

^ Do not interchange connector T2 for Vehicle Speed Sensors -G38- and -G68-.

9. Throttle Position (TP) Sensor -G69-

Location: On the throttle valve housing (engine). Construction can vary, according to engine.

10. Shift Lock Solenoid -N110-

Location: The Shift Lock Solenoid is located on the selector lever.

11. Cruise Control Switch -E45-

Location: On steering column switch.

12. Kickdown Switch -F8-

Location: The Kickdown Switch is integrated in the accelerator cable and is located on the dash panel in the engine compartment.

13. Brake Light Switch -F-

Location: The Brake Light Switch is located on the pedal cluster.

14. Park/Neutral Position (PNP) Relay -J226-

Location: Relay is located on additional relay panel under instrument panel, left.
