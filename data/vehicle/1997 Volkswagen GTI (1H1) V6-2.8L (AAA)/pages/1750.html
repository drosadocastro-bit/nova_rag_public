Central Locking System, Functional Testing



Central locking system, functional testing

Notes:

- If the system is intact, all latches must be locked within approx. 2 seconds. If the central locking system pump and control module - V69- runs longer than 5 seconds, there is a leak in the system.

- If the pump shuts off after approx. 5 seconds and starts up again after 12 seconds, the latch knob was not pushed down on at least one door or the switching point was not reached in the actuator

Requirements

- Doors, rear lid and fuel filler flap closed

- Door windows completely open


Checking


- Push down latch knob on driver's door.

- All latch knobs must go down all doors rear lid and the fuel filler flap should be locked.

- Check locking on every door, rear lid and fuel filler flap.

Note: Central locking and unlocking can be accomplished by operating the driver's door latch knob.

- No anti-theft function
