Alarm Horn: Service and Repair

Located in front left wheel housing, near suspension strut. Locations

Removal and Installation:





- Service Precautions
- Remove front left wheel housing.
- Disconnect 2-pin connector -2- from alarm horn -3-.
- Remove securing nut -1-.
- Remove alarm horn -H8-.
- Install in reverse order of removal.
