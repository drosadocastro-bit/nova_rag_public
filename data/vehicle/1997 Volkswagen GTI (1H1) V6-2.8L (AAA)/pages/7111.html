Hydraulic Oil Level In Fluid Reservoir Too Low

1. Excessive leakage at steering gear (oil in boot), replace steering gear.
2. Excessive leakage at power steering pump (intake connection), replace power steering pump.
3. Leak at fluid reservoir, replace reservoir.
4. Hydraulic oil hose/line defective, replace hydraulic oil hose/line.
5. Leak at hydraulic oil hose/line, tighten hose/line connections.
