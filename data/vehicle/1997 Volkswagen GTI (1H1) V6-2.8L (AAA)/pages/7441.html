Glovebox Removal and Installation




NOTE: As a running change in production starting 03-96, a glovebox was incorporated in the knee bar (with passenger side airbag).

Application - Starting with VIN:
- 49-states TM 093572
- California TM 094505
- Canada TM 095030




1 - Glovebox
2 - Phillips-head screws
3 - Clip

Removing
- Remove Phillips-head screws -2-.
- Pull glovebox downward. Make sure tabs (arrows) disengage.


Installing
Installation is the reverse of removal.
- Verify that clip -3- is in correct position.
