Front Crankshaft Seal: Service and Repair






LEGEND
1 Vibration Damper Bolt
2 Vibration Damper
3 Sealing Flange (Cover) Bolt
4 Front Oil Seal
5 Front Sealing Flange (Front Cover)
6 Cylinder Block
7 Rear Sealing Flange (Rear Cover)
8 Rear Oil Seal
9 Flywheel/Drive Plate
10 Flywheel/Drive Plate Bolts
11 Rear Sealing Flange To Head Bolts
12 Bracket For Coolant Hose.

NOTE: During removal and installation procedures, refer to the component location illustration above and component notes below.

COMPONENT NOTES
Vibration Damper Bolt (1)
- Tighten to: 100 Nm (74 ft. lbs.) + 1/4 turn (90°).
- Always replace.

Sealing Flange (Cover) Bolt (3)
- Tighten to: 10 Nm (7 ft. lbs.).

Front Sealing Flange (Front Cover) (5)
- Coat sealing surfaces with sealer part No. AMV 188 001 02, or equivalent.

Rear Sealing Flange (Rear Cover) (7)
- Coat sealing surfaces with sealer part No. AMV 188 001 02, or equivalent.

Rear Oil Seal (8)
- Remove using removal tool No. 2036 or equivalent.
- Lightly oil sealing lip.
- Install over sleeve tool No. 2003/2A, or equivalent.
- Push in onto stop with installer tool No. 2003/3, or equivalent.

Flywheel/Drive Plate (9)
- When removing and installing, counter holder tool No. VW 553, or equivalent.
- Install drive plate.

Flywheel/Drive Plate Bolts (10)
- Tighten to: 60 Nm (44 ft. lbs.) + 1/4 turn (90°).
- Always replace.

REMOVAL
1. Remove ribbed drive belt.
2. Using counter hold tool No. 3273, or equivalent, to Lock vibration damper, remove vibration damper bolt, then the damper.
3. Unscrew inner part of oil seal extractor tool No. 3203, or equivalent, three turns (approx. 4 mm) out of the outer part and lock with knurled screw.
4. Lubricate threaded head of oil seal extractor, place it in position and while exerting firm pressure, screw it as far as possible into oil seal.
5. Loosen knurled screw and turn inner part against crankshaft until the oil seal is pulled out.

INSTALLATION
1. Lightly oil sealing lip of oil seal.





2. Place guide sleeve from installer tool No. 3266/1, or equivalent onto crankshaft journal.
3. Slide oil seal over guide sleeve.





4. Press in oil seal up to stop with press sleeve from tool No. 3266, or equivalent.
- To do this use an old vibration damper securing bolt.
5. Install vibration damper and lock using counter-hold tool.
6. Tighten new securing bolt to 100 Nm (74 ft. lbs.) and then turn an additional 1/4 turn (90°) further.

NOTE:
- Always use new securing bolt.
- When tightening the securing bolt the crankshaft must not turn.

7. Install ribbed drive belt.
