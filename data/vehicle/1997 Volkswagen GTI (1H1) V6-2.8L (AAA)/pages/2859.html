Clearance Specifications

DIMENSIONS
Oil Pump Backlash:
Wear Limit 0.20 mm (0.0079 inch)
Oil Pump Axial Clearance:
Wear Limit 0.10 mm (0.0039 inch)


TIGHTENING SPECIFICATIONS
Oil Pump Bolt 25 Nm (18 ft. lbs.)
Oil Tube To Block 10 Nm (7 ft. lbs.)

NOTE: Install oil tube bolt with locking fluid "D6", or equivalent.
