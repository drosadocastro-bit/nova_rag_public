096 Transmission

Shifter And Related Components:






CAUTION: Before working on a running engine, position the selector lever at "P" and apply the parking brake.

Before engaging a selector position from position "P" after starting the engine. the brake pedal must be applied.

1 - Transmission

2 - Lever for selector cable

3 - Bracket for selector cable

4 - Washer

5 - Selector lever cable

^ do not bend or kink
^ grease cable ends lightly before installing
^ check that protective sleeve is located properly
^ secure selector cable with clip
^ adjusting: place selector lever at "P" loosen screw of gear lever shaft, set gear lever at "P" (detent must engage parking lock), tighten screw to 20 Nm (14 ft lb)

Note: The front exhaust pipe and dellector must be removed when replacing the selector lever cable.

6 - Shoulder screw - 25 Nm (18 ft lb)

7 - Clip for selector lever cable

8 - 25 Nm (18 ft lb)

9 - 15 Nm (11 ft lb)

10 - Selector support

11 - Gasket always replace

12 - Boot install in support before installing cable

13 - 10 Nm (7 ft lb)

14 - Bushing

15 - Lock lever locks selector lever in "N" or "P"

16 - Spring

17 - Selector lever

^ with locking segment and pressure rod
^ replace complete assembly only
^ assemble before installing

18 - Bushing

19 - Bolt

20 - Clip install after inserting selector cable

21 - Bulb holder

22 - Sleeve

23 - Cover

24 - Program switch - E122

25 - Handle

26 - Set acrew install with locking fluid

27 - Selector lever lock solenoid

^ to adjust or replace switch, detent segment must be removed

28 - 3 Nm (27 in. lb or 31 cm kg) install with locking fluid

29 - Wiring harness for illumination and selector lock solenoid - N110
