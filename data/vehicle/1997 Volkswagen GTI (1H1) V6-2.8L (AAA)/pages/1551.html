Reading Measuring Value Block (Function 08)

WARNING:
- Check the airbag system wiring by visual inspection only
- Do not check continuity or resistance of the airbag igniter circuits.
- Switch off ignition before checking routing of wiring.

V.A.G. Display:





- Press buttons -0- and -8- to select "Read measuring value block" function O8

V.A.G. Display:





- Press -Q- button to confirm input.

V.A.G. Display:





- Select desired display group number and press -Q- button to confirm input.

V.A.G. Display:





If all displays are correct:
- Press -> button.
