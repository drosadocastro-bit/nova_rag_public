Measurement

MEASUREMENT SPECIFICATIONS
Input Shaft Axial Play:
Minimum 0.01 mm (0.0004 inch)
Maximum 0.09 mm (0.0035 inch)

NOTE: If the bearing play cannot be measured, but there is perceptible input shaft play and the input shaft turns freely, the adjustment is acceptable.

Frictional Moment (Turning Torque) At Bearing Preload
Maximum 20 Nm (1.8 inch lb)
