After-Run Coolant Pump: Description and Operation




An additional electric coolant pump circulates coolant whenever the ignition key is ON. The auxillary pump also functions as a part of the radiator cooling fan after-run system and runs automatcially anytime the coolant temperature exceeds 220° F (107° C).
