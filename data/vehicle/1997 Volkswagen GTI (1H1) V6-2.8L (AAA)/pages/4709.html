Vehicle Damage Warnings

HOSE CLAMP REPLACEMENT
- Hoses are secured with spring-type clips. In cases of repair only use spring-type clips for replacements.
- Pliers Tool No. VAG 1921, or equivalent are recommended for removing/installing spring-type hose clamps.
