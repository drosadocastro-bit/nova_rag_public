Brake Light Switch: Adjustments

NOTE: Brake light switch must be removed to adjust.

Adjustments
1. Remove trim under instrument panel
2. Disconnect harness connector at brake light switch
3. Remove brake light switch by rotating 90° to right.
4. Pull brake light switch plunger fully out.
5. Press brake pedal down as far as possible by hand.
6. Guide brake light switch through assembly opening and reinstall by turning 90° to left.
7. Pull brake pedal back up by hand to stop (Brake Light Switch is pushed back by one notch.)
8. Check function of brake light switch.
9. Install instrument panel trim
