Window Washers - Inoperative

Group: 92

Number: 96-01

Date: NOV. 21, 1996

Subject:
Window Washers, Inoperative

Model(s):
Golf, GTI, Jetta 1997 to VIN VM040502

Condition

Inoperative window washer may be due to a kinked hose at one of the following locations:

^ Washer container (Golf, GTI, Jetta)

^ Under driver's seat at B-pillar (Golf, GTI)

^ At hatch (Golf, GTI)

Service

If a vehicle exhibits this condition:

- Assure that washer nozzles are not clogged.

- Check washer hose for kinks at described location(s).

- Re-route any kinked hoses to assure washer fluid flows freely.
