Wire and Fuse Color Code Identification





Wire Color Code Identification
ws = white
sw = black
ro = red
br = brown
gn = green
bl = blue
gr = grey
li = violet
ge = yellow
or = orange

25 A - White

10 A - Red
7,5 A - Brown
5 A - Beige
