Radio/Stereo Noise Filter: Description and Operation



Stereo System, Electrical Interference Suppression

The majority of the electrical consumers/components in the vehicle are suppressed to ensure minimal electrical interference with radio reception.

On vehicles equipped from the factory with a radio or radio prep wiring, the following components are also suppressed:
- Coolant fan -V7-
- Windshield wiper motor -V-
- Rear window wiper motor-V12-

In addition, vehicles with a radio or radio prep wiring will have the following additional Ground (GND) connections:
- Ground (GND) strap from left wheel housing to hood
- Ground (GND) strap from ignition coil -N152- to engine cylinder block (vehicles with gasoline spark-ignition engine only)
