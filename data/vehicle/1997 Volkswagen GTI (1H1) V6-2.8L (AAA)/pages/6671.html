Brake Shoe: Adjustments

Adjusting
The brake shoes are self adjusting.

Adjustment Relieving

Rear Brake Adjustment Releasing:





Use a screwdriver working through wheel bolt hole to push adjusting wedge upward and back brake shoes away from drum.
