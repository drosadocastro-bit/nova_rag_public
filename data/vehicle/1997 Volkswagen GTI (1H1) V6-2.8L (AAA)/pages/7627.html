Multi-Pin Connector I, Part 1, Yellow



Radio connector (Sound System), terminal identification

Multi-pin connector I, -T20-, consists of 3 separate connector parts, each designated by a different color.

Multi-pin connector I, part 1, yellow





1 - Line out left rear, LR

2 - Line out right rear, RR

3 - Ground (GND)

4 - Line out left front, LF

5 - Line out right front, RF

6 - Switched positive (B+ out) for amplifier
