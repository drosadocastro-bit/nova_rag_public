Cabin Air Filter / Purifier: Service and Repair




1 - Frame
2 - Filter element
3 - Plastic nut
4 - Filter housing with gasket

Removing:
- Press filter housing clips in direction of arrows -B- and remove filter element with frame.

Installing:
- Frame must engage in first lamination of filter element (arrow) -A-.
