Manual Transmission/Transaxle: Description and Operation

Cross Section View - Transaxle:




The transaxle case, which contains the transmission gear train and the final drive gears, is a lightweight alloy die casting with an integral flywheel bellhousing. The transmission gear train and the final drive are lubricated by the gear oil in the transaxle case.
