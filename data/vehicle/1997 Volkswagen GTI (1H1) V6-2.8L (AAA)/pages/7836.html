Console: Service and Repair


Vehicles with airbag


Center Console With Airbag:





Removing
- Pull out covers -1- and remove hexhead screws -2-.
- Pull out and disconnect rear lid opening connector -3-.
- Pull cover -A- to rear off center console.
- Remove hex nuts -4-.
- Remove Phillips-head screws -5- (one left-side, two right-side).
- Lift rear end of console slightly and remove from instrument panel.

Installing
- Place spacers -6- on studs.
- Insert console with retaining pins -7- into instrument panel.
- Install center console in reverse order of removal.

Vehicles without airbag

Removing

Center Console Without Airbag:




- Pull out ashtray and remove hexhead screws -6-.
- Pull cover -5- to rear off center console and disconnect electrical connectors.
- Remove hex nuts -4-.
- Remove Phillips-head screws -1- (left and right sides).
- Lift rear end of console slightly and remove from instrument panel.
- Disconnect electrical connectors.

Installing
- Place spacers -3- on studs.
- Insert console with retaining pins -2- into instrument panel.
- Install center console in reverse order of removal.
