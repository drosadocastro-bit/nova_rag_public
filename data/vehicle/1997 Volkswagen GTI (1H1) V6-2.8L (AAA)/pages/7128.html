Steering Wheel With Airbag, Assembly Overview

Steering wheel with airbag, servicing

Airbag functional check and airbag On Board Diagnostic (OBD) program refer to Restraint Systems, Air Bag Systems, Testing and Inspection, Procedures Testing and Inspection

WARNING: The airbag igniter is an explosive device. Handled improperly or without adequate safeguards, it can be accidently activated and cause serious personal injury. To guard against personal injury or airbag system failure, only trained Volkswagen Service technicians should test disassemble or service the airbag system.
Before starting any work on the steering components of an airbag equipped car, read all safety precautions.
