Glove Compartment: Service and Repair



Glovebox, removing and installing





1 - Glovebox

Removing:

- Remove passenger side storage shelf.

- Unclip glovebox light (if present) and separate connections.

- Remove Phillips- head screws - 2 - and hex head screws -4-.

2 - Phillips- head screw

3 - Hinge pin

4 - Hex-head screw

5 - Latch linkage

6 - Gloveboxlid

Removing:

- Remove glovebox -1-

- Remove hinge pins -3- and take lid off.

7 - Latch

8 - Cap

9 - Lock cylinder

- Common key locking can be maintained in vehicle by changing over or attaching locking plates.

10 - Sleeve

- Key must be inserted to remove sleeve.

11 - Phillips- head screw

12 - Cap (glovebox light)

13 - Snap nut
