Trunk/Liftgate Sensor/Switch (For Alarm): Application and ID

Trunk Lock Alarm/Central Locking Switch:





A - Contact switch (Part No. 1H0 953 229)
B - Cam (three stage)
C - Connector (Part No. 191 972 712 B)

NOTE:
- Installing the incorrect contact switch will render the alarm inoperable on vehicles with central locking.
- Part numbers are for reference only. Always check with a Volkswagen Parts Department for the latest parts information.
