Arming and Disarming



WARNING: The manufacturer provides arming and disarming instructions as part of each applicable service procedure.

Read and understand all applicable service procedures and safety precautions completely before beginning work on Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) components or when working near SRS Components or SRS electrical circuits.

Failure to follow all SRS service precautions and procedures may result in damage to the vehicle, personal injury, or death.
