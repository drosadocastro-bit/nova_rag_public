Body Side Molding - Will Not Adhere After Replacement

Group: 66

Number: 99-01

Date: May 6, 1999

Subject:
Body Side Moldings, Installing

Model(s):
Golf, Jetta, Cabrio Nov. 1994 --> 1999

Condition

Extruded body side molding does not adhere after replacement.

May be caused by incorrect procedures used to install molding.


Production

^ Injection molded (black with fastening clips) body side moldings installed through October 1994 production.

^ Extruded body side moldings installed beginning November 1994 production.

Service

When installing extruded body side moldings:

Note:
The working temperature when attaching the moldings must be approx. 20°C (68°F).

- Clean exterior panels with isopropyl alcohol, treat with silicone remover and then rub dry.

- If necessary, warm molding and body surface with hot air blower prior to installation (should be warm to touch).

- Pull protective foil from back of molding and attach molding lightly.

- Press molding on evenly using a roller at approximately 10 lbs. of rolling pressure (one complete pass for each adhesive strip on molding is required).

Note:
Wait at least 24 hours before taking vehicle through car wash.
