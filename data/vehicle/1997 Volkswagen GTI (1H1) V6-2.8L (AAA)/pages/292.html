Oil Pump Disassembly

Oil pump, disassembling and assembling






1. Drive shaft
For oil pump drive

2. Oil pump housing
3. 25 Nm (18 ft lb)

4. Gears
Checking backlash
Checking axial clearance

5. Oil pump cover with pressure relief valve
Opening pressure: 5.3 - 5.7 bar (77 - 83 psi)
Clean strainer if soiled

6. 10 Nm (7 ft lb)
Always replace
Install with locking fluid "D6"

7. Oil pressure pipe
Coat flanges at cylinder block and oil pump housiig with AMV 188001 02 sealant

8. 10 Nm (7 ft lb)

9. 0-ring
