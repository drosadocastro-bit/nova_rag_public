Wiper Blade - Air Deflector Deletion

Group: 92

Number: 96-02

Date: Nov. 21, 1996

Subject:
Wiper Blade, Air Deflector Deletion

Model(s):
Golf, GTI, Jetta 1997 VM036872 -->

Condition

Wiper blades without air deflector.

Production

Air deflector deleted from wiper blade as of
Vin: 3VW_ _ _ 1H_VMO36872.

Wiper blades without air deflector increase life of wiper inserts because pressure (while driving) on wiper arm from the air deflector is eliminated.

Service

Elimination of the air deflector is intentional DO NOT replace wiper blades for this condition.
