Control Module: Service and Repair




REMOVAL

- Release multi-pin connector, then pull connector off of Transmission Control Module (TCM).
- Remove TCM.




INSTALLATION

- Locate multi-pin connector on TCM pins (arrows), then lock multi-pin connector.
