Vehicle Damage Warnings

CAUTION:
^ Always remove some brake fluid from the reservoir before installing new brake pads. When the caliper piston is pushed back, fluid is forced out of the caliper and into the reservoir.

^ After pads are installed, refill the reservoir only to the MAX mark. After replacing brake pads, depress brake pedal firmly several times to seat the pads in their normal operating position.
