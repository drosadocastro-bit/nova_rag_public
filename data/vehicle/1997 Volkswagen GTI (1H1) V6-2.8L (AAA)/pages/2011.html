Headlights, Single Bulb, Servicing (Base Models)

NOTE: Check and adjust headlights after performing any work that may affect headlight aim.

Removing and Installing Headlight Housing:





- Remove radiator grille.
- Disconnect headlight multi-point connector.
- Remove securing screws (arrows) and remove headlight housing forward.
- Install in reverse order of removal.
