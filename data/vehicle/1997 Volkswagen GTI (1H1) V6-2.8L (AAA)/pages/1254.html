Battery Discharges







BATTERY DISCHARGES (Current Draw Suspected)

Test condition: Battery voltage OK

NOTE:
- Use ammeter and/or digital multimeter Fluke 83 (US 1119) for all electrical checks
- Possible causes of battery drain: Corroded and dirty contacts (connectors), Bare wires (stripped insulation), internal short circuits in components.
