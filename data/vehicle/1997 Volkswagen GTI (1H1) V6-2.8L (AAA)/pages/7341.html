Fuse HVAC: Locations

CAUTION: Before working on the electrical system, obtain radio security code and disconnect battery Ground (GND) strap.

NOTE:
- For wiring diagrams and exact fuse and wiring information see Electrical Wiring Diagrams, Testing and Inspection, and Component Locations.
- Components of the R-134a system are identified by lettering R-134a) or by a green label (or stripe).

A/C System Fuses In Fuse/relay Panel:
- S4 - 15A
- S6 - 30A
- S19 - 30A

Coolant Fan -V7- Fuses:
Located in coolant Fan Control (FC) control module -J293-. Note Part No. of J293 for correct replacement.
- S107 - 50A (Coolant fan second and third speed fuse)
- S108 - 20A (Coolant fan first speed and A/C clutch fuse)
