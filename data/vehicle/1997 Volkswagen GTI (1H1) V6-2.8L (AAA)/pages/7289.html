Condenser HVAC: Service and Repair

CAUTION: Immediately plug open connections on A/C components and lines to prevent contamination from dirt and moisture.

Removing:





- Discharge A/C refrigerant system. Service and Repair
- Remove front bumper. Service and Repair
- Remove bumper cross support.
- Disconnect refrigerant lines from condenser -1-.
- Remove screws -2- and remove condenser.

Installing:
Install in reverse order of removal noting following:
- Install seal -3- on both sides of condenser.
- Install condenser and fasten with four screws -2-.
10 Nm (7 ft lb)
- Connect refrigerant lines.
- Upper: 35 Nm (26 ft lb)
- Lower: 15 Nm (11 ft lb)
