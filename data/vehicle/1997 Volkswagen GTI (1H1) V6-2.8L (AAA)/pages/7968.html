Power Sunroof - Close Feature Function Change

Group: 60

Number: 97-04

Date: Nov. 14, 1997

Subject:
Power Sunroof Convenience Close Feature, Function Change

Model(s):
Golf, GTI, Jetta 1997-->1998

Supersedes T.B. Group 60 Number 97-01 dated Feb. 16, 1997

Production
Up to VIN 3VWTD81H7VM070779, power window/sun roof convenience close feature operation:

^ Power windows close, then after 5 seconds, power sunroof closes.

From VIN 3VWTD81H7VM070780, power window/sun roof convenience close feature operation:

^ Power windows and power sunroof close simultaneously.

Beginning m.y. 1998 power window/sunroof convenience close feature operation has changed as follows:

^ Power windows close, then power sunroof closes.

Service
Conditions as described are normal No action required.
