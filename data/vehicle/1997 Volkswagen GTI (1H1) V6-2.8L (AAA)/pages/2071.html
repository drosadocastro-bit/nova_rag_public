Instrument Panel Switches

Instrument Panel Switches:





1- Light switch -E1- For parking lights, headlights and fog lights.
2- Headlight adjuster (Not applicable to USA/Canada)
3- A/C switch -E35-
4- Door lock switch -E150-
5- Rear window defogger switch -E15-
6- Window lockout switch -E39- with left rear window switch -E53- and right rear window switch -E55- (Jetta only)
7- Adjuster for heated driver's seat -E94- and adjuster for heated passenger seat -E95- (Jetta only)
