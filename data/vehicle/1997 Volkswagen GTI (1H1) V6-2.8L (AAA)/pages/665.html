Air Cleaner Assembly




1 - Air Cleaner (ACL) Housing, upper
2 - Air Cleaner (ACL) Element
3 - Air Cleaner (ACL) Housing, lower
4 - O-ring
^ replace if damaged

5 - EVAP Canister Purge Regulator Valve N80
6 - Retaining ring
^ for EVAP Canister Purge Regulator Valve N80

7 - Connector
^ 4 or 6 pin
^ for Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor G70

8 - Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor G70
9 - Intake air duct
10 - Elbow
11 - Hose
^ for Idle Air Control (IAC)
^ from silencer

12 - PCV Heating Element N79
^ for PCV breather
^ arrow on heater element indicates direction of air flow
^ measure resistance at ambient temperature,
approx. 25°C (77°F)
^ resistance: 7 - 12 Ohms.

13 - Connector
^ 2-pin
^ for PCV heating element
^ check Heated Oxygen Sensor (HO2S) heating voltage
supply between terminals 1 and 2 (ignition switched on)
^ specification: approx. battery positive voltage (B+)

14 - PCV Breather Valve
^ note breather hose installation position

15 - To intake manifold, upper
16 - Rubber spacer
17 - retaining ring
