Removal


Carrier Unit Removal



- Remove water drain hoses (arrows) from carrier unit.
- Remove cable drive.
- For easier installation, remove sunroof panel. Removal
- Remove hex bolts and lift carrier unit from vehicle with help of second technician.

Note:

The carrier unit can also be removed complete.
