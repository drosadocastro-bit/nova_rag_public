Power Door Lock Switch: Service and Repair

Removing:





- Using screwdriver, carefully pry out cover plate -A-.





- Insert screwdriver between switch housing and instrument panel.
- Carefully pry switch -150- from instrument panel.
- Disconnect switch connector.

Installing:
- Reconnect switch connector.
- Install switch in instrument panel and push in until fully seated.
- Install cover plate.
