Power Steering Fluid: Service and Repair



Power steering fluid level, checking:


Requirements:

^ Engine at normal operating temperature

^ Vehicle stationary

^ Front wheels in the straight ahead position.

Inspection Service:

Fluid level between "min." and "max." markings.

- Top up with hydraulic oil G 002 000 if necessary.

Note:

If the oil level has dropped to the "min." marking, the power steering should be checked. It is not sufficient simply to top up the hydraulic oil.
