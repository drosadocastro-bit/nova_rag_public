Function 04-Basic Setting



Basic Setting (VAG 1551 Scan Tool Function 04)

NOTE: The "Basic Setting" function 04 is used to bleed the ABS hydraulic unit. Use of the "Basic Setting" function for bleeding is only necessary when at least one brake fluid reservoir is completely empty (e.g. leaks in brake system).

CAUTION:
^ Make sure that filling/bleeding pressure does not exceed 1 bar (approx. 14-1/2 psi) when using a pressure bleeder.
^ For this reason, operating pressure on some filler/bleeder units must be reduced to 1 bar.
^ If the filling/bleeding pressure exceeds 1 bar, there is no guarantee that the ABS hydraulic unit is adequately bled.

1. Connect brake system filler/bleeder unit. Service and Repair

2. Connect VAG 1551 scan tool, switch ignition on, and select "Brake Electronics" address word 03.
