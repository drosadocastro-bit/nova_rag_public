Sunroof / Moonroof Switch: Service and Repair

Sunroof Switch -E8-:





Removing:
- Remove front interior light -W-. Service and Repair
- Remove sunroof switch connector.
- Spread switch retaining tabs (arrows) and remove switch from interior light assembly.

Installing:
- Press switch -E8- into front interior light assembly until all four retaining tabs engage.
- Reconnect sunroof switch connector and install front interior light.
